Building Futures, Nurturing Lives

Our Vision

To empower Tanzanian communities through education, healthcare, sustainable development and the inclusion of all members, thereby ensuring a brighter future for all.

Our Mission

To support young engineers, improve healthcare access for infants aged zero to three months and promote inclusivity in healthcare

About Us

As an extension of SERIS Construction Limited, a distinguished civil engineering company, the SERIS foundation acknowledges and addresses the pressing challenges confronting the community, notably restricted access to quality healthcare, particularly for infants aged zero to three months due to constraints within the national insurance scheme, poses a significant obstacle.Moreover, there is a death of avenues for young engineers to access education , mentorship and career development opportunities.In response to these critical issues the SERIS Foundation mobilizes resources to strengthen healthcare initiatives and facilities the advancement of young engineers.

Why Us

Empowering Young Engineers

The SERIS Foundation is committed to empowering young engineers through tailored career development and mentorship programs to cultivate a skilled workforce driving innovation in Tanzania's construction sector

Improving Healthcare Access

Focusing on infants aged zero to three months, the foundation aims to enhance healthcare accessibility by supporting infrastructure development and advocating for policy changes to ensure essential healthcare services are accessible to all

Community Development

In addition to education and healthcare initiatives, the foundation addresses fundamental needs such as clean water and sanitation to create thriving communities across Tanzania

Our Activities

By implementing these activities, the SERIS Foundation aims to achieve its objectives of empowering young engineers, improving healthcare access for infants, and fostering holistic community development, including climate change mitigation and environmental conservation, across Tanzania

  • 01 Empowering Young Engineers
    • Career Development Workshops

      Organize regular workshops and seminars focused on various aspects of civil engineering, including the latest technologies, project management skills, and sustainable practices

    • Mentorship Programs

      Establish mentorship programs connecting young engineers with experienced professionals in the industry to provide guidance, support, and career advice

    • Internship Opportunities

      Partner with construction companies and engineering firms to offer internships and on-the-job training for young engineers to gain practical experience

    • Scholarships and Grants

      Provide financial assistance for young engineers pursuing further education or specialized certifications to enhance their skills and knowledge

    • Engineering Competitions

      Host competitions and hackathons to encourage innovation and creative problem-solving among young engineers

  • 02 Improving Healthcare Access
    • Antenatal Care programs

      Collaborate with healthcare providers to offer comprehensive antenatal care, including regular check-ups and screenings for pregnant women to identify high-risk pregnancies

    • Neonatal Care units

      Invest in the development and enhancement of neonatal care units in hospitals, ensuring they are equipped with necessary medical equipment and staffed by trained professionals

    • Health Information System

      Develop an online portal and mobile application for registering newborns, tracking their health status, and ensuring they receive timely medical interventions

    • Training Healthcare Workers

      Conduct training programs for healthcare workers to improve their skills in neonatal care, focusing on the management of common infant diseases and emergencies

    • Policy Advocacy

      Advocate for policy changes to improve healthcare delivery and increase funding for neonatal health services, aiming to make essential healthcare accessible to all infants

  • 03Community Development
    • Clean Water projects

      Implement projects to provide access to clean and safe drinking water in underserved communities, including the installation of wells and water purification systems

    • Sanitation Facilities

      Construct and improve sanitation facilities in rural and urban areas, promoting hygiene and reducing the incidence of waterborne diseases

    • Community Health Education

      Conduct awareness campaigns and educational programs on the importance of hygiene, sanitation, and preventive healthcare practices

    • Infrastructure Improvements

      Support the development of essential community infrastructure, such as schools, health centers, and community centers, to enhance the overall quality of life

    • Climate Change and Environmental Conservation

      Initiate and support projects aimed at mitigating climate change and promoting environmental conservation. This includes tree planting initiatives, promoting renewable energy usage, and educating communities on sustainable practices to reduce their carbon footprint

    • Partnership and Collaborations

      Work with local and international organizations to leverage resources and expertise in implementing community development projects effectively
